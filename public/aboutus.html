About Us

Hey there! Yes, you! Want to learn more about how we work here at Homework Help Now? Your at the right place! From our tutors to our founding, find it all here!

How we Started

Founded in 2019, Homework Help Now was made as a technology fair entry for the school. v1 was first deployed on November 12, 2019. It was quite rough, using very basic bootstrap and no domain name. (!) A few weeks later, the site got first place at the school-level. I decided that the site needed a partial revamp; this became known as v1.5. A month later, the revamp was completed, just in time for the county competetion. I luckly placed in first in the county. I was moving onto the state competetion. Months later, while Covid-19 started its deadly siege over the United States, I competed in the tech fair virtually. I ended up placing 3rd place in my state. I then went on a bit of a hiatus from this site until recently, where I found the time to redo this site from scratch, ditching bootstrap and moving to mdbootstrap. Now, as your reading this, we have finished, almost a year from winning in the school competetion. I am very grateful for being able to participate in that competetion.

Our Tutors

Yes, the question you've been waiting for! How do we get our tutors? Actually, all of the tutors are my friends, some from where I used to live in California, some from where I live now. All of us are taking classes 8th grade and higher, including a few 10-11th grade classes. If you aren't sure we can help you, give us a email and we can try our best to help you. All of our tutors are volunteers, so make sure to thank them for helping you!

How to find a Tutor

If you need to find a tutor, you have 2 methods. You can go to our Homework Help page and find the correct category you need help in. You can also click the live-chat button at the bottom of the screen, and if one of our operators is online, you can get helped, live!
