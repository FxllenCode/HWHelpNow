Why we created Homework Help Now

Ethan created Homework Help Now after spending a night trying to find a true source that can help you with your homework. After finding nothing, he teamed up with his freinds to start the site. Our long term vision is to add many more tutors from diffrent states. The site was created by Ethan Webber as a tech project. All tutors are volunteers who are giving up a bit of time in their day to help other students in need. They come from two diffrent states, GA and CA. We offer one-hundred percent free homework help to all students, grades K-9th. We currently can help you in four diffrent subjects. They are ELA, Math, Social Studies, and Science. We also have a live chat feature, so you can get help with your homework without waiting a day or two. On behalf of all of the tutors, we hope that you enjoy our site!
